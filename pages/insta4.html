Step-by-Step Download Guide

Downloading Instagram Reels

  1. Open Instagram and find the reel you want to save
  2. Tap the 3-dot menu in the bottom right corner
  3. Select "Copy Link" from the menu
  4. Visit InstaDownloder4U
  5. Paste the link in the input field
  6. Click "Download" and select HD quality

Example

To save @natgeo's wildlife compilation reel: Copy the link (instagram.com/reel/Cx7m8l1vPjZ/), paste it into our downloader, and get the HD video file.

Saving Instagram Stories

  1. Find the story you want to save
  2. While viewing, tap the 3-dot menu at the bottom
  3. Choose "Copy Link"
  4. Paste into our downloader tool
  5. Download as MP4 video or JPG image sequence

Note: For private accounts, you can only download your own stories.

Archiving Instagram Highlights

  1. Go to the profile with the highlight
  2. Open the highlight
  3. Press and hold on any story frame
  4. Select "Copy Link" from the pop-up menu
  5. Our tool will detect all highlight stories and offer batch download

Downloading Feed Photos/Videos

  1. Find the post in your feed
  2. Tap the 3-dot menu above the post
  3. Choose "Copy Link"
  4. Paste into our downloader
  5. Select between original image or compressed version
